ARCHITECTURE RESULT

Background WAS being remounted per slide. Each of the 5 renderSlide functions wraps content in OnboardingLayout, which rendered OnboardingBackground inside SafeAreaScreen. The outer render used key={currentSlide} which forced full unmount/remount on every slide change, resetting all animations.

Fix: OnboardingBackground now renders once at the top-level return, outside the key={currentSlide} wrapper. OnboardingLayout's SafeAreaScreen uses transparent background so the persistent orbs show through from behind.
